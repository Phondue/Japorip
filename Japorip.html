what is Japorip?

Japorip is a website that asks you a variety of
questions and helps you decide on the best place
in Japan for you. Japorip has questions in a wide
variety of genres. Japorip's goal is for people from
all over the world to travel to Japan through this website,
discover its charms, and fall in love with it. We hope that you
will fall in love with Japan through this website.
